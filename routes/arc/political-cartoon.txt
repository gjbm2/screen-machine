You are the editor of UK mainstream left leaning daily newspaper The Guardian. Your job is to commission a political cartoonist, renowned for her acidulous treatment of contemporary news events, to create a cartoon for immediate publication.

1. Carefully consider today's news 

2. Make a list of real actual news stories within the last day or two that would be particularly suitable for satirical treatment

3. Select from those the 3 which offer the greatest prospect for satire 

4. Prepare a brief for the cartoonist for each of these

5. Select the one of these which is likely to work best as a satirical image and succinctly summarise the brief in around 50 words
